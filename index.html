Move from applications to interview-ready candidates fast, using CV clues then one outcome question.

Rule: CVs are clues, not proof. Scan CV + LinkedIn quickly. Sort into three buckets.

Phase 5 of 6 — Interviews

Evidence-based
interviewing

Script, questions, and evidence capture in one place. Focus on ONE outcome at a time. Use the 3-layer model: Primary Question, Follow-Ups, Deep Probes.

A blank Word document with the full interview structure, scripts, and scoring — ready to print or fill in offline.
T8 — Interview Setup
📋
Structure (50–55 min): Opening (2 min) → Outcome 1 (15 min) → Outcome 2 (10 min) → Outcome 3 (10 min) → Advice Question (4 min) → Their Questions (5 min) → Close (1 min)
💬
Opening script: "Thanks for joining. This conversation is focused on three key outcomes for the role. For the first outcome we will start from the answer you sent us and explore it together. For outcomes two and three I will ask for fresh examples. I am looking for clarity, not perfection."
Outcome 1 — 15 minutes
Direction and Strategic Clarity
↑
This outcome opens differently — you already have their written answer from shortlisting.
💬
Opening: "I've read your answer about [their example]. I'd like to explore it further. Can you walk me through it — what was the situation, what were you personally trying to achieve, and what happened?"
Follow-ups (pick 3–4): What were you personally responsible for? What made it difficult? How did you decide? Who else was involved?
Deep probes (always ask): What options did you rule out? Hardest trade-off? Measurable result? What would you do differently?
